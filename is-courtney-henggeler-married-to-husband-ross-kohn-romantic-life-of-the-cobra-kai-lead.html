Is Courtney Henggeler Married To Husband Ross Kohn? Romantic Life Of The Cobra Kai Lead

June 2024 · 3 minute read
When Courtney Henggeler got married to her husband, Ross Kohn? If you are keen to explore the blissful married life of the actress, then stay tuned with us.

Courtney Henggeler came to prominence with her prominent role, Amanda LaRusso, in the Netflix comedy series Cobra Kai. She is also notable for portraying Sheldon Cooper’s twin sister, Missy, in The Big Bang Theory.

Meet Courtney Henggeler Husband, Ross Kohn!

Courtney Henggeler’s husband, Ross Kohn, is an American actor and producer. He made his acting debut with a role, Jason, in the romantic drama sci-fi film Jack & Bobby, in 2005.

Ross further acted in a mystery crime drama series, Veronica Mars, as Jack Artman in 2006. He then appeared as William in Michael Buran and Anthony Gaudioso’s thriller film, Medium, in 2007. His other movie credits include Autopsy, Re-Cut, and Duke.

As a producer, Courtney produced a made-for-television movie, Escape from Polygamy, in 2013. Furthermore, he worked as an executive producer in other films like Starving in Suburbia, Lethal Seduction, and American Typecast.

Regarding Ross’ personal life, he has been a loving husband a responsible father. He and his wife, Courtney, tied the knot at The Bedford Post Inn, New York, on October 10, 2015. Moreover, the couple’s wedding was an intimate ceremony.

The pair got the bundle of joys after becoming parents of two adorable children. They welcomed their first child, a baby boy, Oscar Noah, on January 25, 2017. Later, they announced that they were expecting their second child in January 2019.

The family of three turned out to be four when Courtney gave birth to a daughter, Georgie Healy Kohn, on March 1, 2019. Courtney and Ross are currently raising their two kids in a healthy environment at home based in NY.

How Much is Courtney Henggeler Net Worth?

Courtney Henggeler maintains a decent net worth of $1 million as of 2021. She earns $100k per episode in the martial arts comedy-drama series Cobra Kai.

Her husband boasts a lucrative net worth of $2 million like Courtney. He currently makes a huge profit from his filmography works with his production company.

A Short Introduction to Courtney Henggeler Family!

Courtney Healy Henggeler was born on December 11, 1978, in Phillipsburg, New Jersey. Her parents, Mr. and Mrs. William Henggeler, are the secret to her success. They always encouraged her to pursue her dream job.
