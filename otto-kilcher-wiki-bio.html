Otto Kilcher: Wiki, Bio, Age, Net Worth, Wife, Kids

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Otto Kilcher is one of the lead actors of ‘Alaska: The Last Frontier’ which airs on the Discovery Channel. The show has been gaining viewers continuously since the day it started airing. It is based on the life of the Kilcher family, particularly as they try to survive the harsh climate of the Alaskan wilderness.

They also face the cold winters of Alaska without any heating facilities and services. The Kilcher family displays hunting and survival skills which attract many viewers.

Alaska The Last Frontier Otto Kilcher Net Worth

Otto Kilcher’s net worth reportedly sits at $4 million. His wealth comes from his exploits in the field of mechanics. On the show, we get to see Otto display a wide range of skills, which are vital for increasing his net worth. He always had a keen interest in this field.

Another primary source of income obviously comes from television and his business activities. He rose to fame on the reality show ‘Alaska: The Last Frontier. Therefore, this show is also a vital factor in boosting his bank account.

Otto Kilcher’s Married Life and Wife Charlotte

Otto Kilcher has been married three times His first serious relationship was with Olga Von Zegasar. Unfortunately, this marriage ended within a few years. Next in line was Sharon McKemie. Despite the fact that they had two sons together, their marriage still ended.

Presently, he is with Charlotte Irene Adamson, who he has been with for the past two decades. The couple leads a happy life together.

Charlotte was once a vegetarian, but because of the need for survival, she started eating meat again. She now takes care of food supplies such as eggs, honey, and milk. This ensures that the family has sufficient food to survive the dangerous winters of Alaska.
