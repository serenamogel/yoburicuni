Meet Paul Bettany’s Parents And Children

Paul Bettany was born to Anne (née Kettle) and Thane Bettany in London, England. His parents eventually divorced. The Bettanys are a family of entertainers. Anne, his mother, was a theatrical vocalist, instructor, and stage manager. Thane, his father, was also a dancer, actor, and drama instructor. He was also Sophie, Countess of Wessex’s godfather. Thane passed away in 2015, and Anne has retired from performing.

The family stayed on the school grounds when his father taught at Queenswood School, an all-girls boarding school near Hatfield. Paul was reared as a Roman Catholic, however, his church attendance waned following his confirmation. With his father, he eventually tried with numerous different Christian groups, including Methodist and Church of England congregations. The actor has declared himself an agnostic. His ethnicity is English, according to Ethnicelebs.

Howard John Bettany and Irene Edith Bryan were Paul’s paternal grandparents, while Leslie/Lesley Harry Kettle and Olga M. Gwynne were his maternal grandparents. Lesley was a musician and promoter, and Olga was a well-known actress.

Paul’s Siblings and Childhood

Paul Bettany had two siblings: Sarah, his sister, and Matthew Bettany, his late brother. His elder sister, Sarah, is a writer. Paul’s niece has a daughter called Emaly Bettany. Matthew, their younger brother, perished after he fell onto concrete from a tennis pavilion roof at Queenswood when he was eight years old. Paul was 16 years old at the time. Paul dropped out of school, moved away, and became a street performer in London. He lived in a modest apartment and made money by playing his guitar.

After a few years, he acquired a new work at an old home. Paul enrolled in the Drama Centre in London after working there for a year. Paul was born on May 27, 1971, and his zodiac sign is Gemini.

Jennifer Connelly and Paul Bettany have three children

Paul Bettany is married to Jennifer Connelly, an American actress. On January 1, 2003, the couple married in Scotland. They met while co-starring in the 2001 film A Beautiful Mind. They did, however, date after a time since they were both in previous relationships. Paul had a crush on Jennifer as a youngster after watching her in the 1986 picture Labyrinth. The couple has two children: Stellan, 20 years old, and Agnes Lark, 12 years old. He is also Jennifer’s stepfather from a prior relationship with photojournalist David Dugan.
