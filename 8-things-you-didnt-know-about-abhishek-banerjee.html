8 Things You Didnt Know About Abhishek Banerjee

August 2024 · 2 minute read

➡ Abhishek Banerjee Biography

An Indian actor, Abhishek Banerjee who is known for his additional skills, has worked on many film projects. Also, he is best known for playing the role of Janana in the horror-comedy film Stree which was released in the year 2018. Apart from this, he has given stellar performances in numerous films and web series. However, his fans still want to know more about him and here we are sharing some unknown things they did not know.  

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Abhishek Banerjee

1- Additional Skill

Abhishek Banerjee is known not just for his acting skills, yet he is also known for being a casting director.

2- Hobbies

Apart from acting, Abhishek Banerjee loves listening to music and also loves traveling in his leisure time.

3- Famous for

The actor is famous for the role of Vishal “Hathora” Tiyaagi in Paatal Lok which was released in the year 2020.

4- Bollywood Debut Film

The actor made his Bollywood debut film “Rang De Basanti” starring Aamir Khan. The film was released in the year 2006.

5- Film Casting Associate Project (Debut)

In 2010, the most talented actor Abhishek Banerjee worked on another film named “Once Upon a Time in Mumbai” as a casting associate.

6- Casting Director (Debut)

In 2011, the actor added another feather on his cap as he worked as a casting director on the film named “Dirty Picture” starring Emraan Hashmi, Vidya Balan, and Naseeruddin Shah.

7- Favorite Book

He is an avid reader and one of his most favorite books is “Terribly Tiny Tales” by Penguin Books India.

8- Favorite Actors

Abhishek Banerjee admires the Bollywood veteran actor Amitabh Bacchan, Rajinikanth, and Ranbir Kapoor.
