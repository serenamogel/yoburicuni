Career

When RJ Neena first started her profession, she was turned down for Radio Jockey interviews. One voice coach said that her voice is unsuitable for FM radio programming. But she didn’t give up and continued trying. Her commitment to becoming one of Kerala’s radio jockeys brought her success. Neena never looked back once she was given the opportunity to host a program with K.J Yesudas, a renowned vocalist. Neena’s sense of humor and ability to observe many issues make her an engaging RJ. Her quips frequently make her listeners chuckle.

She was once a member of Club FM, but after two years of service, Neena transferred to Mango 91.9 FM. She has been in contact with Mango for over a decade. She has been invited to numerous events as a motivational speaker. Through her program, she became the first RJ to expose the globe to the world’s first transgender RJ Makeup artist Ananya. She never misses an opportunity to assist society via her profession, and she has a qualification as a social worker from Bharath Sevak Samaj.

Getting fame in the industry provided an opportunity for Neena to appear as a guest on the TV shows show ‘Nayika Nayakan’ in Mazhavil Manorama. Neena started practicing acupuncture methods after becoming a qualified Acu-healer at Bharathiyar University. She learned acupuncture when her backbone was severely wounded in an accident, which was eventually repaired with acupuncture treatment.
