Abhishek Sharma, popularly known as Krushna Abhishek who is a film and TV actor, born on 30th May 1983, hailing from India. He has appeared in a large number of movies, TV shows, and serials. The most talented actor is also known for his comic skills as he appeared in many comedy movies. In 2014, he appeared in the movie “Bol Bachchan” for which he gained much recognition. Moreover, he is an award-winning actor who has been constantly winning the hearts of many.

Career

In 2002, Krushna Abhishek started his career with a movie named “Yeh Kaisi Mohbat Hai” and since then, he appeared in several movies. Some of his movies include “Hum Tum Aur Mother” which was released in 2005, “Jahan Jayega Humen Paayega” released in 2007, and others that are worth sharing to watch.

Moreover, he has worked in several TV shows and serials including “Jhalak Dikhla Jaa” the dance reality show in which he appeared in season 4 in 2010. Also, he appeared on “Nach Baliye-3” in 2007. While he also appeared in some comedy shows, like; “Comedy Circus” in 2008 also was part of Comedy Circus 2 and 3 in 2009.

Krushna Abhishek participated in dancing reality shows, starting with Kabhi Kabhii Pyaar Kabhi Kabhii Yaar and Nach Baliye together with his girlfriend Kashmira Shah, and ultimately won the first one.

He also starred in another dancing reality show, Jalwa Four 2 Ka 1. In the year 2010, he partook in a dancing reality show titled Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa with famous choreographer Robin Merchant. Beforehand, he had also starred as a judge in a dance reality show aired on DD National, Krazzy Kiya Re, together with Sudha Chandran.

In 2010, he also appeared in “Comedy Circus Ka Jadoo” and “Jubilee Comedy Circus
in 2011. And you will be amazed to know that Krushna Abhishek also appeared on “The Kapil Sharma Show -2” in 2018. While he got much appreciation for the performance in the movies “Entertainment” and “Bol Bachchan” released in the year 2014.

As a celeb couple, Krushna Abhishek and his wife took part in a reality show titled “Love Lock Up” aired on UTV Bindass in the year 2011, wherein famous actress Priyanka Chopra played the crucial role of a peacemaker.

Achievements

Among various prestigious achievements, he won the Indian Telly Award for the category of Best Actor in a Comic Role for Comedy Circus in 2014. Also, he was awarded the Indian Television Academy Award for the role of Popular Comedy-Duo alongside Sudesh Lehri for Comedy Circus in 2015 and the list goes on.
