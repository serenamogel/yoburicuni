What is John Mulaneys zodiac sign and what does it say about him?

February 2024 · 3 minute read

John Mulaney has opened up about many aspects of his life in his latest Netflix comedy special, Baby J. In it, he talks about his struggles with addiction and his time in rehab, as well as touching on his former relationship with Anne Marie Tendler. It’s safe to say that the comedian has had a chaotic couple of years that have changed him. His comedy special starts by emphasizing how he has been working on himself. But we still want to know more; what does Mulaney’s sudden shift mean? Could it be something to do with his star sign? Probably not, but it’s fun to speculate.

For those wondering, Mulaney’s sun sign is Virgo. Virgos are typically hard-working and intelligent individuals with a fear of underperforming. There’s no doubt that Mulaney is hard-working; he’s had one hell of a successful year with his role in Puss in Boots: The Last Wish, as well as his well-received comedy special. However, he’s also admitted to having feelings of anxiety, which could be down to his Virgo personality manifesting.

Virgos also feel the need to find an outlet for their skills and talents. Mulaney has certainly found himself an outlet as he’s not only been a successful comedian for a decade but he’s branched out into voice acting and already found himself involved in major Hollywood productions such as Spider-Man into the Spider-Verse. Generally, Virgos feel most content when they feel useful and busy, which Mulaney has certainly been doing for the past couple of years.

John’s moon is also in Sagittarius, which typically means the individual is happy-go-lucky and easy-going, oftentimes not making plans and more just going with the flow. Their cheerfulness and optimism are catchy and easily infect others. You could probably say that a comedian is an ideal job for a lunar Sagittarius.

Communication is Key

Mercury, the planet of communication, also rules over Virgo. Those with Mercury in Virgo typically think about things more logically and practically, which from the looks of things, Mulaney has been doing more so recently. After his time in rehab, he’s taken time for self-reflection and rebuilt himself from the ground up. He’s not afraid to talk about his past issues, as he’s chosen to discuss them in depth multiple times rather than sweep them under the rug.

John Mulaney is undoubtedly very in tune with himself and his own thoughts. Whether he’s knowingly acted in accordance with his zodiac sign or not, he has spent the last couple of years working on himself and his communication, all of which are factors that can help Virgos flourish. Hopefully, he continues to do so after the success he’s seen this year.
