Biography

Philippa Hanna was born in England on March 29, 1984. British songwriter, singer, and guitarist best known for her work in the Contemporary Christian Music genre. She has released three studio albums since her 2007 debut, and has toured alongside Wet Wet Wet and Rebecca Ferguson as well as numerous solo tours. She shares management with Daughters of Davis, fellow Sheffield act The Gentlemen, and US poet Peter Nevland. She has also toured alongside Lionel Richie. Prior to working as an independent musician, she auditioned for a number of talent contests, including being on the reserve list for the BBC’s Fame Academy.
